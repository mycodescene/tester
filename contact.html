Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services

What types of products do you trade?

+

We specialize in construction materials, automotive products, heavy machinery, agricultural commodities, scrap metals, and provide comprehensive logistics services. Our portfolio includes everything from cement and steel to luxury vehicles and premium spices.

Which countries do you serve?

+

We serve clients in over 45 countries across North America, Europe, Asia, Africa, and Australia. Our global network allows us to source products from the best suppliers and deliver to virtually any destination worldwide.

How do you ensure product quality?

+

We work only with certified suppliers and conduct rigorous quality inspections at every stage. All products come with quality certificates and compliance documentation. We also offer third-party inspection services upon request.

What are your payment terms?

+

We offer flexible payment terms including Letters of Credit (L/C), Telegraphic Transfer (T/T), and other secure payment methods. Terms are customized based on order value, client relationship, and specific requirements.

How long does shipping typically take?

+

Shipping times vary by destination and product type. Ocean freight typically takes 15-45 days, air freight 3-7 days, and land transport 5-15 days. We provide detailed timelines and tracking information for all shipments.

Do you provide after-sales support?

+

Yes, we provide comprehensive after-sales support including warranty services, spare parts supply, technical assistance, and maintenance programs. Our support team is available to assist with any post-delivery requirements.
