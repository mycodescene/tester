About Global Trade California

Building bridges across continents through trusted trading partnerships

Our Story

Founded in 2009 and headquartered in the heart of California, Global Trade California has emerged as a leading force in international trading, connecting businesses across continents with premium products and reliable supply chain solutions. Our journey began with a simple vision: to bridge the gap between global suppliers and businesses seeking quality products at competitive prices.

Over the past 15 years, we have built an extensive network of trusted partners spanning 45+ countries, specializing in diverse sectors including construction materials, automotive trading, heavy machinery, agricultural commodities, and scrap metal recycling. Our multi-sector expertise allows us to serve as a one-stop solution for businesses looking to expand their global reach or source premium products from international markets.

What sets us apart is our commitment to quality assurance, competitive pricing, and end-to-end supply chain management. From initial procurement to final delivery, we handle every aspect of the trading process, ensuring our clients receive exceptional value and peace of mind. Our California headquarters serves as the strategic hub for our global operations, leveraging the state's position as a gateway to international markets.

Today, Global Trade California stands as a testament to the power of strategic partnerships, innovative logistics solutions, and unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction. We continue to expand our global footprint while maintaining the personalized service and attention to detail that has made us a trusted partner for businesses worldwide.

Our Vision

To become the world's most trusted and innovative global trading partner, connecting businesses across continents while fostering sustainable economic growth and long-term partnerships that transcend borders.

Our Mission
